At Krishi Mandala, we empower farming communities to encourage collaborative thinking to further drive workplace innovation. By closing the loop and leveraging ethical farming methods, we help businesses grow organically and nurture a consumer first mindset.
The main objective of this project is to connect farmers directly with their customers. This may help farmers to get sufficient money without any intermediates. And also helps customers to get fresh products and at low price directly from farmers. Static data visualization helps farmers to predict the price of a particular crop from previous year and also helps farmers to grow crops more efficiently with the help of information (videos, blogs) provided.
